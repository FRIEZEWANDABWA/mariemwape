Rejoindre NOGEC

En juillet 2025, Marie Mwape Kashimbo a rejoint NOGEC (Organisation Nationale pour la Bonne Gouvernance et le Changement), marquant son entrée formelle dans l'arène politique pour plaider en faveur d'une meilleure gouvernance et de politiques sociales.

Travail de Plaidoyer

Sa plateforme politique se concentre sur le bien-être social, la réforme des soins de santé, l'accès à l'éducation et la création de politiques qui protègent les populations les plus vulnérables de la société congolaise.

Social Welfare

Comprehensive social protection programs for vulnerable populations including orphans, disabled individuals, and elderly citizens.

Healthcare Reform

Accessible healthcare systems with focus on mental health services and support for people with disabilities.

Education Access

Quality education opportunities for all children, with special programs for marginalized communities.

Good Governance

Transparent, accountable governance structures that prioritize citizen welfare and human dignity.

Objectifs Politiques

Œuvrer pour une réforme sociale globale, l'amélioration des systèmes de santé et des structures de gouvernance qui privilégient la dignité humaine et la justice sociale.

Short-term Goals (2025-2026)

  • Establish policy framework for vulnerable population protection
  • Advocate for increased healthcare funding in Tshopo province
  • Build coalition for social welfare reform

Long-term Vision (2027+)

  • Comprehensive social protection system nationwide
  • Universal healthcare access with mental health focus
  • Transparent governance structures at all levels

"From Philanthropy to Politics"

"My journey from founding the FMMPS Foundation to joining NOGEC represents a natural evolution of my commitment to serve. Through politics, I can amplify the voices of those I've worked with for years and create systemic change that reaches beyond individual assistance."

- Marie Mwape Kashimbo
